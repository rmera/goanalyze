    Shape indicators

    goAnalyze can obtain indicators of the global shape of a PyMOL selection. The percentage of planarity and elongation are derived from the properties of the moment of inertia tensor, based on the work by Taylor et al., .(1983), J Mol Graph, 1, 30

    To obtain the percentage of planarity and elongation for a selection in a single frame, use:

    goshape sele

    Where sele is the name of the selection.

    If you want to follow the evolution of the shape of one or more selections along a trajectory, you need to use gomd. Use:

    gomd planarity, sel1, sel2 ...

    or

    gomd elongation, sel1, sel2 ...
